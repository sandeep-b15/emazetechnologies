    INDUSTRIAL LASER PROJECTOR

    Industrial Laser Projector are the optical/Light guidance system, which allows to work without templates or markers, eliminate tape measurement and provides complete step to step guidance to the workers throughout the complete manufacturing and assembly process. The optical angle covering upto 800X800 mm allows large covering areas which can also be increased by adding multiple laser projectors together to cover very large and complex parts especially for large part application in Aerospace Industry, Ship building, Heavy Engineering etc. Laser projectors provides more accurate positioning of parts and also with quick visual inspections.

    DIGITAL AUTOMATION SYSTEM

    Optimize your Quality Control by implementing Digital Automation Systems. This system consists of Large tolerance display allows error free and quick decision making to Digital Gauges operators. Automatic shop floor data collection feature is an another advantage of the system not only saves huge of time but also provide you precise results. The Statistical Process control feature helps you in quick data analysis and quality control. You can also opt for multi gauging system since our solution is compatible to all type of digital gauges.

    2D DRAWING AUTOMATION

    The 2D Drawing Automation software offered by us is for Integrated Computer Aided Inspection processes. Software enables you to handle your complex 2D Drawings easier and faster. Basic Quality Assurance processes like ballooning is no more challenge using this software. Besides create your customized report according to your requirements and formats. Supports all type of file format for Inspection reports to quickly analyze the deviations in drawing. Also capable to import reports from any measurement device.

    LASER MARKING & ENGRAVING

    We offer Laser Marking & Engraving machine. Permanent, durable markings are of the utmost importance when it comes to traceability in high heat environment as of aerospace industries, automotive industries and other manufacturing industries. Since you can't rely on traditional marking methods like inkjet, labeling, RFID & stickers. Our laser marking machine ensures permanent marking in very short time with the marking speed of 12000 mm/sec. You have more design flexibility with zero operating costs, thus leads your brand to next level.

Additive manufacturing offers opportunities for more complex design and reduced delivery times. High performance materials and parts, produced faster and with the design flexibility of 3D Printing.
